Honey Pancakes

Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine 1 cup of flour, 2 tablespoons of sugar, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
Add Wet Ingredients: Whisk in 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of melted butter until smooth.
Heat a Pan: Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
Cook Pancakes: Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden.
Serve: Serve warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.

Veggie Burger

Prepare Patties: Mix 1 lb of ground beef with salt, pepper, and any preferred spices. Form into patties.
Cook Patties: Grill or pan-fry patties over medium heat until they reach desired doneness.
Toast Buns: Lightly toast burger buns on the grill or in a pan.
Assemble Burger: Place patty on the bottom bun, add cheese (optional), lettuce, tomato, and onions.
Serve: Top with condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo) and the top bun, and serve with fries or a side salad.

Salmon Fry

Marinate Fish: Marinate fish fillets with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and a pinch of turmeric for 15-20 minutes.
Prepare Batter: Mix chickpea flour (besan), rice flour, chili powder, and water to form a smooth batter.
Coat Fish: Dip the marinated fish into the batter, ensuring an even coat.
Heat Oil: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
Fry Fish: Fry the fish until golden and crispy on both sides. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
Serve: Serve hot with lemon wedges and tartar sauce. .

Noodles

Cook Noodles: Boil noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
Prepare Veggies: Slice carrots, bell peppers, and cabbage. Mince garlic and ginger.
Stir-Fry: Heat oil in a wok, add garlic, ginger, and vegetables. Stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes.
Add Noodles: Add cooked noodles to the wok, along with soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar.
Toss: Toss everything together until evenly coated and heated through.
Serve: Serve hot, garnished with spring onions.

Samosa

Prepare Dough: Mix 2 cups of flour, 1/4 cup of oil, and a pinch of salt with water to form a firm dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
Prepare Filling: Boil and mash 2 potatoes. Mix with peas, cumin seeds, coriander powder, and garam masala.
Shape Samosas: Roll dough into circles, cut in half, and fold into cones. Fill with potato mixture and seal edges.
Heat Oil: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
Fry Samosas: Fry samosas until golden brown and crispy.
Serve: Serve hot with tamarind or mint chutney.

Dal Makhni

Soak Lentils: Soak 1 cup of whole black lentils and 1/4 cup of kidney beans overnight.
Cook Lentils: Pressure cook the soaked lentils and beans with salt and water until soft.
Prepare Tadka: In a pan, heat butter and add cumin seeds, chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, and tomatoes. Cook until soft.
Add Spices: Add turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala to the tadka.
Simmer: Add cooked lentils to the pan, stir in cream, and let it simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
Serve: Garnish with butter and fresh cream, and serve hot with naan or rice.

Shahi Paneer

Prepare Gravy: Blend onions, tomatoes, cashews, and a few spices into a smooth paste.
Cook Paste: In a pan, heat oil and cook the paste until it thickens.
Add Paneer: Cut paneer into cubes and add to the gravy. Stir in cream.
Season: Add garam masala, cardamom powder, and salt to taste.
Simmer: Let it simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes.
Serve: Garnish with fresh cream and coriander leaves, and serve with roti or rice.

Paneer Tikka

Prepare Marinade: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
Marinate Paneer: Cut paneer into cubes and marinate in the yogurt mixture for at least 30 minutes.
Skewer Paneer: Thread paneer onto skewers, alternating with onions and bell peppers.
Grill: Grill the skewers on a preheated grill or bake in an oven until charred.
Baste: Baste with butter or oil during grilling for extra flavor.
Serve: Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
